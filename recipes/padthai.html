Pad Thai

Pad Thai for cold nights

Pad Thai, just the best you could say for really any time.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Fish Sauce
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Sugar
  • Lime
  • Peanuts
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Soak noodles in warm or boiling water
  2. Combine fish sauce, sugar, and vinegar in a bowl and set aside
  3. Prosper
